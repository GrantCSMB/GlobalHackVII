Information On Mentors

Our process to find the right mentor, the Goals for the Mentorship programs and helpful topics to discuss with your mentor...

Finding the Right Mentor

When you begin using our website, you can tell us some more about yourself, and we'll be able to pair you with a mentor that can relate to your experiences. Our management will include workers who have gone through what many immigrants are going through, and will have already been down the path that these foreign born individuals are on. We will help guide others into their new environment while adjusting to the culture and living a comfortable, sustainable life.

Setting Goals With Your Mentor

As your mentor gets to know you more and you develop relationships, your mentor can help you set goals for your adaptation into society. Not only can they help you learn about your new culture and community, but they can help you adjust. They can help give you things to accomplish, allowing you to learn how to function independently.

Important Questions

  • Was there a time where you felt like you failed? What would you have done looking back?
  • What are important strengths to have and how can I expand these skills?
  • Looking back, how did you envision and then shape your career and future? How has this planning ahead influenced your life today?
  • How do you want to expand your future still? Where do you see yourself in five years?
  • What are the obstacles you face today and how do they compare to those you faced?

Contact a Mentor Now

Our mentors have already been down the path you're on now, and have experience that can be applicable to you. They'll help answer any questions you have.
